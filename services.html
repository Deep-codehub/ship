Our Services

At NVAN Logistics, we offer a wide range of logistics solutions tailored to your business needs. We have the expertise and resources to handle your logistics requirements efficiently and securely. Explore our services below:

Road Freight

Our road freight solutions ensure the safe and timely delivery of your goods across the country. With an extensive fleet and experienced drivers, we guarantee efficiency and reliability.

  • Nationwide Coverage
  • Full Truckload (FTL) and Less-than-Truckload (LTL)
  • Real-time Tracking
  • Cost-effective Delivery Solutions

Air Freight

For time-sensitive shipments, our air freight services offer a fast and reliable solution. We partner with major airlines to ensure your goods are delivered quickly and securely.

  • Global Reach
  • Express Services
  • Customs Clearance Support
  • Air Cargo Insurance

Sea Freight

Our sea freight services are ideal for bulk shipments. Whether you're shipping to or from overseas, we offer flexible and cost-effective solutions for containerized cargo and more.

  • FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less-than-Container Load)
  • Global Shipping Network
  • Reliable Transit Times
  • Competitive Pricing

Warehousing & Distribution

Our state-of-the-art warehouses are designed to handle your storage needs, while our distribution solutions ensure that your goods reach their destinations in perfect condition and on time.

  • Inventory Management
  • Order Fulfillment
  • Pick & Pack Services
  • Real-time Inventory Tracking

Supply Chain Management

We offer end-to-end supply chain management services, optimizing every step from procurement to final delivery. Our technology-driven solutions allow you to manage your supply chain more efficiently.

  • End-to-End Visibility
  • Demand Forecasting
  • Logistics Optimization
  • Cost Reduction Strategies

Customs Brokerage

Our experienced customs brokerage team ensures smooth and hassle-free customs clearance for your international shipments. We handle all the paperwork and regulations to make sure your goods move quickly across borders.

  • Import/Export Customs Clearance
  • Tariff Classification
  • Compliance with Local and International Regulations
  • Customs Documentation and Paperwork
